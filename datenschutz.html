Datenschutzerklärung (Privacy Policy)

This website is a personal portfolio of tattoo artist Andrii Borysiuk. It serves to present his work and provide contact information. The site does not use forms, tracking, or analytics tools on its own server. No personal data is collected by this website itself.

Contact options are provided via links to WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and a button that copies the email address. By using these links, your data may be processed by the respective platforms according to their privacy policies. Andrii Borysiuk / MADE WITH PAIN is not responsible for the data processing practices of these third-party services.

The embedded Google Maps block shows the location of the studio where appointments are held. By interacting with the map, Google may process your data in accordance with its privacy policy. MADE WITH PAIN does not collect data from the map itself.

This website does not set cookies or track visitors. Please note that external services (Google Maps, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram) may use their own cookies when you interact with them.
